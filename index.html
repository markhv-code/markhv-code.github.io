About Me

I love creating things, I've always had that passion since I was young. I dreamed of future technologies when I was a kid and let my imaginations run wild.

My passion for creativity and technology first led me to audio engineering which primarily consisted of producing audio books as well as recording music. Looking for a new challenge I completed my bachelor's degree and worked in the financial services industry with a large wealth management firm. I'm grateful for all the opportunities that I've had but I still didn't feel like I had found what I was really looking for in my career.

Desiring something that aligned closer to who I am, I continued searching and when I came across software engineering I lit up. I dove right in and started taking a course in C++ and got hooked. Then I enrolled with App Academy and once I started building full-stack applications I was elated. I love building software and take pride in my craft and look to continue perfecting my abilities.
