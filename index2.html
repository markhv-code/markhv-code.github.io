About Me!

As a young man, Mark Valdez enjoyed being a part of the Boy Scouts of America, receiving the most prestigious award in Scouting, at the age of 15 (only 1% of Scouts achieve). For the next two years after high school, Mark was a full-time representative for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Los Angeles, California. During that time, he worked 11 hour days teaching life improvement lessons. After only three months, Mark was chosen to lead other groups of representatives in improving teaching and productivity techniques. Mark Valdez graduated from Brigham Young University—Hawaii, one of the nation’s most diverse universities. One of his projects, Mark edited website content for Vizzda, one of the largest commercial realty databases in South Florida. Afterwards, the Co-Founder of Vizzda said, “The students of this class not only exceeded my expectations by literally architecting the messaging and content of our new homepage, but the feedback your students offered was fantastic.” Mark is currently a volunteer at the Surfrider Foundation and is passionate about ocean preservation and protecting the environment.
